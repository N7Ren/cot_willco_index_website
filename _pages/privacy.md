## 1. General Information

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. We treat your personal data confidentially and in accordance with the statutory data protection regulations and this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Data Collection

When you visit this website, certain information is automatically collected and stored in server log files. This typically includes:

- Browser type and version
- Operating system used
- Referrer URL
- Host name of the accessing computer
- Time of the server request
- IP address

## 3. Cookies and Analytics

This website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not use our own cookies for tracking purposes. However, third-party services like GitHub or external link partners (e.g., Amazon, Whop) may use their own cookies when you click on links.

## 4. Affiliate Links

This website contains affiliate links. When you click on these links and make a purchase, the partner service (Amazon, Whop, etc.) may collect data to track the referral. This is necessary for the billing of commissions.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to receive information about the origin, recipient, and purpose of your stored personal data at any time free of charge. You also have a right to request the correction, blocking, or deletion of this data.
