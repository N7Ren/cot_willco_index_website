3. Nature of COT Data (The “Lag” Factor)

Users must acknowledge that the Commitment of Traders (COT) reports issued by the CFTC are lagged data. The information released on Fridays typically reflects market positions held as of the preceding Tuesday. Market conditions may have changed significantly between the data collection date and the release date. This data is a measure of past sentiment and is not a real-time indicator of future price movements.
